Project and Portfolio Ideas 2021

Posted on Dec 6, 2021 2 mins read

The year’s almost over and holiday time off is coming for many developers. Of course, the most important thing to do with time off is relax, have fun, and spend time with the people you care about. Taking breaks is essential to good mental health and productivity over the long term. But if you (like me) can’t go very long without some kind of side project, or if you’re itching to try out a new technology over the break, feel free to take one of my ideas below and run with it. I certainly won’t have time to do all of them.

PhD On Demand

The pitch: Cameo for people with doctorate degrees.

The idea: Ever wondered how many planets the average star system has? Or why we can’t use giant magnets to launch rocket ships? Or if a gene mutation could give someone the ability to see infrared light? Or why you always get a cold the day before Thanksgiving? Or how new prescription drugs are discovered? You could Google these things, but sometimes what you really want is a face-to-face explanation by someone who’s spent their life studying a subject. Download this app and you can scroll through a list of researchers, scientists, professors, journalists, and graduate students who will gladly post a video response to any of your nerdy questions. Prices start at $10 USD per video.

How to monetize: Keep a 10% cut of everything users pay.

Required skills: Mobile app development, video capture and cloud storage, identity verification

Home Office Ergo

The pitch: Upgrade to a home office that looks nice and doesn’t ruin your back.

The idea: Home offices are more popular than ever. Unfortunately, most people haven’t put much effort into designing a home office that’s healthy, ergonomical, and joyful to be in. (I know I haven’t.) Sign up at Home Office Ergo and a consultant will give your home office an in-person assessment for health and lifestyle factors, delivering a written report with several personalized recommendations. For an add-on fee, they’ll do a complete home office makeover.

How to monetize: Take a cut of consultants’ fees.

Required skills: Invoices and billing, content marketing, affiliate marketing, conflict resolution
