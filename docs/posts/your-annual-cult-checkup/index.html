Your annual 'Am I in a cult?' checkup

Posted on Oct 14, 2021 One min read

Consider this:

  • Most cult members don’t think they’re in a cult.
  • You don’t think you’re in a cult.
  • Therefore, it’s possible you’re in a cult.

Now, I have no reason to suspect that anyone I know is in a cult. The elephant in the room here is Mormonism, the religion I was raised in, and honestly I think some Mormons are cultists and others are not. I’ll leave that one for people to decide on their own. But there are many types of cults, and some are harder to recognize than the religious kind: there are political cults, one-on-one cults (a term coined by Dr. Janja Lalich), get-rich-quick cults, nutritional cults, motivational speaker cults, and so on. And cults are easy to get sucked into, especially if you’re easily excited like I am.
